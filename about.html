FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about MediCare Plus services and policies

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment in three ways: (1) Visit our main website and use the online booking system, (2) Call us directly at +1 (800) 123-4567, or (3) Visit our facility in person. We offer appointments within 48-72 hours and accept same-day emergency appointments.

What insurance plans do you accept?

MediCare Plus accepts insurance from all major providers including Medicare, Medicaid, Blue Cross, Aetna, United Healthcare, and many others. We also offer flexible payment plans for uninsured patients. Please contact our billing department for specific details about your insurance plan.

Do you offer telemedicine consultations?

Yes! We offer 24/7 telemedicine consultations for most services. Virtual consultations are perfect for follow-ups, prescriptions, and non-emergency concerns. You can schedule a video appointment through our website or contact our scheduling team for assistance.

What are your operating hours?

Our regular clinic hours are Monday to Saturday, 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM. We have a dedicated emergency department that operates 24/7/365. After-hours urgent care and emergency services are always available. Holiday schedules may vary.

How long is the average wait time for appointments?

We pride ourselves on minimal wait times. Most patients are seen within 15-20 minutes of their scheduled appointment time. Emergency cases are prioritized and triaged immediately. We understand that your time is valuable and strive for efficiency.

Do you have pediatric services?

Absolutely! Our pediatric department specializes in care for infants, children, and adolescents. We offer wellness visits, immunizations, developmental screenings, and specialized treatment. Our facilities are designed to be child-friendly and welcoming for families.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring: (1) A valid photo ID, (2) Insurance card or proof of payment method, (3) Any recent medical records from other providers, (4) A list of current medications, (5) Completed health history form (available on our website). Arriving 10-15 minutes early helps us process your information smoothly.

Are your doctors board-certified?

Yes! All our physicians are board-certified in their respective specialties. Many of them hold advanced certifications and are members of national and international medical associations. You can view detailed credentials for any doctor on our website's doctor profile section.

How do you handle patient privacy and medical records?

We strictly adhere to HIPAA regulations and maintain the highest standards of patient privacy. Your medical records are secure and confidential. You can request copies of your records at any time. We use modern, encrypted systems to protect your personal and health information.

Do you have wheelchair accessibility?

Yes, our facility is fully accessible for individuals with disabilities. We have wheelchair ramps, accessible restrooms, elevators, and specially designed patient rooms. If you need specific accommodations, please mention this when booking your appointment.
